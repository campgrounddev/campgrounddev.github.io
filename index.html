Positioning

Campground’s mission is to give communications professionals the best resources anywhere to connect with the people, publishers, and platforms that can best amplify their message.

We’re a team of seasoned marketers, engineers, and data scientists with extensive experience in public relations work and a 15+ year track record of building and sustaining successful companies. We built Campground to solve our own problems first and have direct experience in identifying the primary market opportunities within the professional communications and media intelligence software space.

Market Differentiators

  • Auto-generated Outreach

    Campground generates highly-tailored media outreach contact lists and strategies based on relatively simple user input, guiding communications professionals through each step with a powerful, intuitive, engaging UI.

  • State-of-the-art architecture

    The core application runs on a graph database architecture and extracts dozens of media intelligence data points from each news article, including the use of proprietary natural language models and integrated knowledge graphs.

  • Deep domain expertise

    As PR veterans and leaders of a creative digital agency, our team has intimate knowledge of the space and the problems we’re trying to solve. Our presence and experience in the industry make us unique among our competition.

The best lists anywhere

We started our company with a simple mission: Connecting communications professionals to media contacts better than anyone else does. Campground is a small, self-funded team; we want to be the best in the industry at a few big things rather than "just OK" at many things, and there is no greater opportunity in the PR software space than creating comprehensive solutions for finding the right media outreach targets quickly. Based on user research and product testing, we estimate that Campground can massively scale team productivity and outreach quality, reducing about 6 hours of work for an associate communications professional down to 20-30 minutes.


The market opportunity

The marketing software was benchmarked around $50 billion in 2020 (with a projected CAGR of 17.4%), and we estimate our media intelligence product can directly address 20% of the market. Outside of the primary media intelligence platform, we also see revenue paths via a paid API for businesses to use our natural language models, boutique PR consulting, and an expansion of the platform to cover more aspects of the digital public relations space, such as press release generation and distribution, automated email with tracking and metrics, SEO analytics, and customer relationship management.
