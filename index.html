Positioning

Campground’s mission is to give communications professionals the best resources anywhere to connect with the people, publishers, and platforms that will best amplify their message.

We’re a team of seasoned marketers, engineers, and data scientists who come from a place of deep experience with public relations work and with a 15+ year track record of building and sustaining successful companies. We built Campground to solve our own problems first and know first-hand the extensive market opportunities within the professional communications and media intelligence software space.

Market Differentiators

  • Auto-generated Outreach

    Campground generates highly-tailored media outreach and contact lists and strategies based on relatively simple user input, guiding communications professionals through each step with a powerful, intuitive, engaging UI.

  • State-of-the-art architecture

    The core application runs on a graph database architecture and extracts dozens of media intelligence data points from each news article, including the use of proprietary natural language models and integrated knowledge graph.

  • Deep domain expertise

    As PR veterans and leaders of a creative digital agency, our team knows the space and the problems we’re trying to solve. Our foothold and experience in the industry make us unique among our competition.

By the numbers

Campground is build on a deep, rich database of media insights gleaned from news articles.
We aim to provide the most useful, accessible, and comprehensive insights on the market.

  • 130M News Articles
  • 115,000 Journalists
  • 3 Billion Data Points
  • 1 Million New articles/week
  • $10B T.A.M.

We started by addressing the biggest opportunity we saw in public relations: Connecting communications professionals to media contacts better than anyone else. Campground is a small, self-funded team; we want to be the best in the industry at a few crucial things rather than "just OK" at many things, and nothing is more lacking in the PR software space right now than comprehensive solutions for finding the right media outreach targets quickly. The marketing software industry sat at around $50 billion in 2020 (with a projected CAGR of 17.4%) and we estimate our media intelligence product can directly address the needs of 20% of that. Outside of our platform, Campground also sees revenue paths via a paid API to use our natural language models (which are 96% accurate to Google’s for multilabel classification), boutique PR consulting, and an expansion of the platform to cover more aspects of the PR space: Press release generation and distribution, automated email, SEO analytics, and customer relationship management.
